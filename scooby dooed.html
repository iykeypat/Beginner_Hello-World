WELCOME!
This program replaces all consonants at the start of a word until it runs into a vowel
with an "r" (Just like Scooby Doo). For example, the word "scooby" would become "rooby" and “xylophone” would become “rophone”.

NOTE:
Numbers and special characters such as '-', '%' are
not directly 'scooby dooed'. Rather, they are implied
to be 'scooby dooed' as you pronunce them.
